About the Band

Harvey Dale

Harvey Dale is the band's 23-year-old lead guitarist and singer. Harvey has always known he would be a rock star, but he could have never predicted the talent of his bandmates.

Sammy Garza

Renowned drummer Sammy has been one of Harvey's lifelong friends. She is credited for developing the band's punk/grunge style. She is notorious for her pink hair and viral TikTok videos.

Kris "KT" Laine

KT is a self-taught bassist who began playing at the age of 12 after her parents insisted she learn an instument for her church's worship team. She met Harvey and Sammy in college when the pair was looking for someone to play a backing track on their first album.
